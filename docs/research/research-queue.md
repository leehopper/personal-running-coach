# Research Queue

Topics to hand off to a deep research agent. Each entry captures the question, why it matters, and where findings should feed back into the planning docs. Full research artifacts live in `research/artifacts/` — only outcomes and key takeaways get pulled into the main planning documents.

## How to Use

1. Add a topic below with a clear research question and context on why it matters.
2. Hand off to a deep research agent.
3. Store the full output in `artifacts/` (e.g., `artifacts/competitive-landscape.md`).
4. Summarize key takeaways back into the relevant planning doc and update status here.

Research is batched by dependency. Later batches depend on findings from earlier ones.

- **Batch 3 (Important but not blocking POCs):**

## Errata

Concise index of known inaccuracies in prior research artifacts. Each affected artifact also carries an inline errata note at the top of its file.

- **R-019 (`batch-9a-daniels-pace-table-verification.md`)** — Did not verify VDOT 30–49 cell values; "likely correct" conclusion was based on step-size consistency alone. A 2026-04-14 audit found residual VDOT 49→50 anomalies in Interval (+1.55 pp) and Repetition (+3.69 pp) after the DEC-040 row-shift fix. Superseded by the equation-based direction in DEC-042.
- **R-025 (`batch-11-daniels-implementation-patterns.md`)** — Two claims corrected: (1) "I-pace = 100% VO₂max" — actual fixed constant is 97.3%, per R-028; (2) R-pace = predicted mile race pace — disputed by R-028's 3K-with-multipliers formulation, final resolution pending R-035. Pattern 1 / Pattern 3 recommendation is still valid.
- **R-028 (`batch-12c-pace-zone-derivation-constants.md`)** — R-pace derivation via 3K race prediction × distance-specific multipliers (0.9295 / 0.9450 / 0.9528) is attributed to a LetsRun reverse-engineering rather than first-party source and conflicts with R-025's mile-race-prediction formulation. Final resolution pending R-035. T = 88.0% and I = 97.3% constants are unaffected and remain authoritative.
